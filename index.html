TSI Official Cricket Lawbook

Codified System Override Framework — Preceding Alternate Local Regulations

1. General Core Principles

All operations strictly enforce the official ICC Spirit of Cricket parameter metrics. Any form of behavioral dissent or administrative subversion towards officiating staff triggers immediate multi-match penalty metrics under Match Referee reviews.

2. Strategic Over-Rate Caps

Short format blocks are timed explicitly at 4.5 minutes per over. Fielding systems failing to deliver the opening delivery of their final over parameter inside allotments proceed under a strict penalty of dropping one mandatory defensive fielder inside the ring perimeter.

3. Adaptive Playing Conditions

  • No LBW Bounds: Leg Before Wicket dismissals are completely suspended. Stance obstruction monitoring remains strictly monitored under umpire panels.
  • Mankad Warning Requirements: Bowlers must issue a single formal verbal caution directly to non-strikers before performing a valid Mankad run-out execution.
  • Wicket-keeping Stumpings: Legitimate clean stumpings and hit-wickets are strictly validated across game profiles.
