About Tigers Sports Inc.

Founded on foundational community principles, TSI works actively to bridge social development through athletic tournament operations. We structure safe, competitive environments that foster inclusion, build long-term development strategies for youth pathways, and champion community harmony.

Strategic Pillars

01

Social Integration

Providing foundational sports platforms to integrate ethnic communities smoothly into the broader New Zealand ecosystem.

02

Tournament Delivery

Operating structural frameworks, elite refereeing, digital statistics engines, and robust rulebook compliance models.

03

Community Welfare

Promoting physical wellbeing, religious values, strict codes of sportsmanship, and mental health structures through team sports.

Tournament Lawbook Framework

All TSI activities are bound under structured playing codes combining official ICC lawbooks with local adaptive tournament standards.

01. Match Duration Quotas

Standard tournament play operates tightly under an 8-over or 10-over per-side format. Slow over-rates result in immediate defensive ring placement penalties.

02. Equipment & Safety Regulations

Only verified tennis-balls wrapped in white insulating electrical tape are authorized. Use of fiber-bats remains completely prohibited across leagues.
